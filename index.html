TENET SYSTEMS How It Works
Four-engine architecture

Built for institutional
grade from first principles

Engine 01

Orchestration

Conducts every other engine. Owns all order state. The only component that writes to the clearing ledger.

Engine 02

Reservation

Dual-gate race-condition guard. Locks FI vault before LP routing begins. Locks LP inventory after fill plan confirms. Chain-aware deadlines.

Engine 03

Execution

Price discovery across LP vaults. Pareto elimination. Blended cost ranking. Greedy fill up to 5 legs. Asset delivery via aggregation vault.

Engine 04

Settlement

Extracts FI payment simultaneously with delivery. Routes LP payout via CCTP, internal netting, or Everclear. Funds never disappear.

Moat 01

Clearing Ledger as Proprietary Data

Every order builds an immutable record — institutional digital asset flow, pricing, LP depth, settlement patterns across all chains. A complete record of who traded what, when, at what price. Irreplicable by any later entrant. Value compounds with every transaction.

Moat 02

Bilateral Network Effects

Every FI onboarded increases order flow for LPs. Every LP onboarded improves price discovery for FIs. Both sides compound together. Switching away means losing access to the other side's network — a cost that grows with every participant added.

Moat 03

LP Lock-In via Settlement Certainty

Native chain settlement is a contractual guarantee. LPs build treasury workflows, Fireblocks integrations, and reconciliation systems around Tenet's reliability. Operational switching costs grow with tenure. This is structural stickiness, not artificial lock-in.

Moat 04

Proprietary Inventory Path

10bps on every order accumulates as Tenet's capital reserve. Over time Tenet becomes both orchestrator and liquidity provider — capturing the full spread on high-volume orders, not just the routing fee. The long-term margin expansion flywheel.
